Joe at work.

Hi, I'm Joe. I'm a senior software developer in the Boston area, focusing on games.

I mostly work with Unity and C#. Other tools I use include Unreal, Pixi, Javascript / Typescript, the Adobe suite, Autodesk 3ds Max, HTML, WebGL, Node, and many more as the situation demands.

Since 2007, I have developed and contributed to a number of code projects in the course of creating mobile, console, and PC games, other software, and in making animation for film, web, broadcast, advertising, etc.

Since 2015, I have been exclusively working as a developer, and in a senior role as a team lead since November 2016. Previously, I split my time as an animator and technical artist. I briefly describe many of my projects here.

Outer Wilds

2020-2021

Unity, C#, 3ds Max, Maya, Maxscript

Worked on the Nintendo Switch port of award-winning game, Outer Wilds.

Through Tricky Fast, we partnered with Unity themselves to provide support and Unity Engine expertise to Mobius Digital, the developer.

  • I developed editor tools for artists and designers to optimize original assets for the Switch.
  • I made several integrations that would pass data back and forth between Unity and Digital Content Creation tools, and corresponding scripts in those tools.
  • I also developed monitoring and reporting tools to keep track of statistics with regard to these optimizations.
  • I worked closely with Unity's engineers as well as the original developers.
  • I contributed to the technical design of further optimizations for the existing systems to accommodate the Switch, such as the unique challenges the planet system posed in object culling.
